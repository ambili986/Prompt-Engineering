# Prompt: Compare Two AI Responses

You are given two AI-generated responses to the same question.

## Task:
Evaluate both responses and determine which is better.

## Evaluation Criteria:
- Accuracy
- Clarity
- Completeness
- Instruction adherence
- Tone suitability

## Constraints:
- Provide comparison in bullet points
- Keep explanation under 120 words
- Do not repeat content from responses
- Be objective and concise

## Output Format:
- Preferred Model: A or B
- Reasoning (bullet points)
- One improvement suggestion
